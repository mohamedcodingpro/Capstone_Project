Dementia Status & Advice

Advice for Demented Individuals

It's important to stay mentally and physically active. Engage in memory exercises, and make sure to have a healthy routine. Also, make sure to stay socially connected with friends and family.

General Health Advice

Keep maintaining a balanced lifestyle. Eating well, exercising regularly, and having good sleep habits help in keeping your mind sharp and body healthy.
